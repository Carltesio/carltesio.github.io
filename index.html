Gait Analytics

A complete end-to-end system for real-world gait and foot load analysis. AI powered

We design and develop a complete gait analysis ecosystem — from hardware to data intelligence. The platform is built to capture meaningful biomechanical data during natural movement, enabling analysis that extends beyond controlled laboratory or clinical settings.

Our system includes smart insoles with embedded pressure sensors, wireless communication with mobile devices, real-time data collection during everyday movement, cloud-based storage and processing, and advanced analytics for gait, load distribution, and movement patterns.

The result is a portable and scalable “gait lab” for real-world conditions — a system designed to bring objective movement intelligence into rehabilitation, performance, research, and product development.
