Carlos brings close to two decades of expertise across two distinct but deeply connected fields. His early career was forged in the world of wearable product development — living and working in Italy and China, immersed in both research and the demanding realities of manufacturing. That rare combination of hands-on engineering and international experience shaped how he thinks about products: not just what they do, but how they are made and for whom.

Today, Carlos works at the heart of enterprise software development within large-scale organisations, building systems that must be robust, scalable, and seamlessly integrated into complex technical landscapes. He has moved fluidly between roles — from Senior Software Developer to Product Owner.

What drives him is not technology for its own sake, but the real value it creates for the people who use it. The instinct for human-centred thinking, combined with deep technical depth, is what makes his work endure.
